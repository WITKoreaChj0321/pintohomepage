About PINTO Take a picture of your precious moments

Your Private Studio,
Your Way

PINTO is a self photo studio located in Daegu Technopolis, South Korea. During your reservation, you have exclusive use of the entire studio — no rush, no strangers, just you and your memories.

Choose between black & white or color. All original photos are provided for free after your session. The studio is fully equipped with a changing area, full-length mirror, hangers, and various props.
